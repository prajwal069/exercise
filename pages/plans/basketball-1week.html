Basketball 1 Week Warm-Up Plan

Day 1: 10 min jog, dynamic stretches, basketball drills
Day 2: 10 min jog, shooting drills, footwork exercises
Day 3: 15 min jog, agility drills, layup practice
Day 4: 10 min jog, defensive drills, ball handling
Day 5: 12 min jog, passing drills, shooting drills
Day 6: 10 min jog, sprinting drills, conditioning
Day 7: 15 min jog, full court drills, cool down stretches
