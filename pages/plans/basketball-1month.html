Basketball 1 Month Warm-Up Plan

Day 1: 10 min jog, dynamic stretches, basketball drills
Day 2: 15 min jog, shooting drills, footwork exercises
Day 3: 12 min jog, defensive drills, ball handling
Day 4: 10 min jog, passing drills, agility drills
Day 5: 10 min jog, layup practice, conditioning
Day 6: 15 min jog, sprinting drills, strength exercises
Day 7: 12 min jog, scrimmage practice, cool down
Day 8-14: Repeat the first week plan, with slight variations in drills
Day 15-21: Include advanced drills like shooting off the dribble, pick and roll
Day 22-28: Full-court practice, integrate strength training and conditioning
Day 29: Rest day with light stretching
Day 30: Full basketball game simulation, cool down, review progress
